About Me

See Résumé

Hi! I’m Ruth, a fourth-year undergraduate student at the University of Toronto, specializing in Mathematical Applications in Economics & Finance with a major in Statistics. I thrive at the intersection of math, economics, data, code, and curiosity.

Currently, I work as a Research Assistant with Professor Avi Goldfarb, analyzing real-world data, supporting machine learning experiments, and structuring large datasets to uncover insights in academic publishing and innovation ecosystems.

Outside the classroom, I enjoy playing video games, Stardew Valley being my favourite! and handwriting. I also love my cat as much as I love myself; she’s a furry, unfettered, smart girl who keeps life interesting.
